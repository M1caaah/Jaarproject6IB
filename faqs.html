Key/Gift

Why do I get an email instead of a key instantly?

To enhance security and ensure a smooth and reliable delivery process, we have implemented a system that sends activation keys directly to your email.
This approach adds an extra layer of protection to your purchase, reducing the risk of unauthorized access or potential issues associated with instant delivery.

By delivering the activation key through email, we can also provide you with a convenient record of your purchase for future reference.
We understand the importance of a secure and seamless experience, and this method allows us to prioritize both security and customer convenience.

Payment issues

Why did something go wrong with my payment?

If you encounter difficulties with your payment, various factors may contribute to the issue.

The problem might arise from a timeout in the payment gateway, cancellation by your bank, accidental cancellation on your part, or technical glitches with the payment gateway.
If this problem persists across multiple orders, we recommend reaching out to either your bank or the payment provider for assistance. Specifically, for credit card payments, ensure that 3D verification is enabled, as it may be necessary for the payment provider to process your payment. Consider trying a different payment method to see if that resolves the issue.

If the payment was deducted for the order, a refund should be processed by the payment provider soon. To inquire about the transaction, please contact your payment provider for further assistance.

Our merchants

Where do we source our keys?

At Bytebazaar, transparency and authenticity are at the core of our values. We take pride in ensuring that every activation key is sourced directly from the developers or authorized distributors of the respective products. This direct relationship guarantees that you receive genuine, untampered keys, free from the influence of unnecessary intermediaries motivated solely by profit.

By obtaining our keys directly from the developers, we not only prioritize the security and legitimacy of our offerings but also contribute to fair business practices. This approach allows us to support the developers behind the products and maintain a clear and ethical supply chain.

We are committed to providing you with the highest quality products and services, and our direct relationship with developers reflects this dedication. If you have any questions or need further clarification, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team. Your trust and satisfaction are our top priorities.
